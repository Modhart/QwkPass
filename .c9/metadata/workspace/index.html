Pricing

\n

Various payment plans for all kinds of travelers

\n

The QwkPass tag will be available to all users who wish to get paperless at the terminals and enjoy the benefits of speeding through the terminals to board their transit vehicle. \n

\n

1 Tag = $9.99

\n

Commuter Benefits

\n

With QwkPass, everyday commuters will be able to enjoy the benefits of discounted subscriptions for public transit use.\n

\n

The everyday commuter

\n

will be discounted 15% on all rides during a 2 hour windows that the user can split and shape to their schedules.\n

\n

The part time commuter

\n

who commutes no more than 4 days will be able to enjoy 10% on all rides during a 2 hour window.

\n

The sporadic commuter

\n

who commutes at least 2-4 days will be able to enjoy 8% on all rides during a 2 hour window.
