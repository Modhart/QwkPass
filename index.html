QwkPass

With QwkPass, you will be able to navigate your way through any public transit system without confusing payments, exhaustive schedule planning between multiple transit types and with the addition of our QwkPass tags, public transportation more efficient and pain free allowing the user to access these services faster than ever before without the fear of missing that train

Features

Improving Transit Travel Efficiently

Mobile App

Easy mobile app to manage subscriptions, account balance, and allow user to enter any metro system with just your phone or QwkPass tag

Trip Planning

QwkPass has a real-time trip planning feature to automatically create an easy to follow travel guide in any language that you require

QwkPass Tags

QwkPass Tags allow anyone to fastrack their boarding times effortlessly and conveniently without wasting countless time paying for access

QwkPass Terminal

Expertly designed terminals at stations to fastrack the process of accessing train stations with the partnered use of our QwkPass tags

How it works

Easy to use and made for the traveler

Step 1

Route your trip using the QwkPass Trip Planner
Step 2

Arrive at your starting destination
Step 3

Access the transit service using either the QwkPass mobile app or the QwkPass Tag
Step 4

Enjoy your trip!
