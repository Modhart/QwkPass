With QwkPass, you will be able to navigate your way through any public transit system without confusing payments, exhaustive schedule planning between multiple transit types and with the addition of our QwkPass tags, public transportation more efficient and pain free allowing the user to access these services faster than ever before without the fear of missing that train

Features

Improving Transit Travel Efficiently

Easy mobile app to manage subscriptions, account balance, and allow user to enter any metro system with just your phone or QwkPass tag QwkPass has a real-time trip planning feature to automatically create an easy to follow travel guide QwkPass Tags allow anyone to fastrack their boarding times effortlessly and conveniently Expertly designed terminals at metro stations to accurately read user data through RFID technology and communication with back-end server in order to authorize payment

How it works?

Easy to use and made for the traveler

 

Step 1

Route your trip using the QwkPass Trip Planner
Step 2

Arrive at your starting destination
Step 3

Access the transit service using either the QwkPass mobile app or the QwkPass Tag
Step 4

Enjoy your trip!

Pricing

Various payment plans for all kinds of travelers

The QwkPass tag will be available to all users who wish to get paperless at the terminals and enjoy the benefits of speeding through the terminals to board their transit vehicle.

1 Tag = $9.99

Commuter Benefits

With QwkPass, everyday commuters will be able to enjoy the benefits of discounted subscriptions for public transit use.

The everyday commuter

will be discounted 15% on all rides during a 2 hour windows that the user can split and shape to their schedules.

The part time commuter

who commutes no more than 4 days will be able to enjoy 10% on all rides during a 2 hour window.

The sporadic commuter

who commutes at least 2-4 days will be able to enjoy 8% on all rides during a 2 hour window.
